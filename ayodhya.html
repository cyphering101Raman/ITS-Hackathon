RamMandir

Ayodhya's antiquity is deeply rooted in Hindu mythology and the epic Ramayana. The Ram Janmabhoomi, believed to be the birthplace of Lord Rama, has been a site of religious importance for centuries. The Babri Masjid, built in the 16th century, became a focal point of religious and historical significance, leading to a complex and historic legal dispute.
